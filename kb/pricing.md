## Plans

- **Starter** — $29/mo. Single user, email support, 3-day response SLA.
- **Growth** — $99/mo. Up to 5 users, priority email + chat support, 1-day
  response SLA.
- **Scale** — $299/mo. Unlimited users, dedicated Slack channel, same-day
  response SLA, quarterly business review.

## Billing policy

- Monthly plans can be cancelled anytime; no refund for the partial month
  already billed.
- Annual plans are refundable pro-rata within the first 30 days only.
- Failed payments trigger a 3-day grace period, then account pause (not
  deletion) until payment succeeds.
