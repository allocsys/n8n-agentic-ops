## Response time targets

- Starter: 3 business days
- Growth: 1 business day
- Scale: same business day

## What counts as urgent (human escalation required)

- Payment failures affecting active service
- Security or data-privacy concerns
- Explicit cancellation or refund requests
- Any message expressing anger or threat of churn

Urgent messages are never auto-replied to by the AI agent — they are ticketed
and routed to a human, per the escalation carve-out documented in
`PROCESS.md`.
